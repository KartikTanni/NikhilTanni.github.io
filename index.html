NRITTA   (1-3-2018)

Nritta is an extraordinary dance performance by hundreds of dancers. It is one of the most awaited and delightful event of CHRIST, where experience of dance with a beautiful theme brings, memorable day of art. Nritta- 2017 was a record breaking event with 600+ bringing the theme, PANCHATTAVA, meaning five attributes of reality, to life and delighting the audience with a phenomenal performance. Nritta-2018 will try to push the envelope in the field of Dance and show put on a breath-taking show.

STEPHAN DEVASSY   (2-3-2018)

Stephen Devassy is a pianist and keystar player hailing from Palakkad, Kerala. Mr. Devassy is an alumnus of Trinity College, London, where he earned the record of Asia’s highest scorer in solo piano exams. He has worked with musicians like Rahman, Hariharan, Ustad Amjad Ali Khan, L Subramaniam and Zakir Hussain.

DEVDAN CUP

An inter-collegiate sports fest organised by CHRIST, Devadan Cup presents an opportunity to budding sportsmen to battle it out on a level playing field. With tournaments in several sports including, cricket, football, basketball, volleyball and handball, Devadan Cup promises to be a legendary championship both on the turf and on the court.
